A cyberpunk-styled exercise reminder app that helps you stay active throughout the day.

**Live at [buz2er.com](https://buz2er.com)**

## Features

- **Periodic Reminders**: Get notified every 15-90 minutes (configurable)
- **Exercise Tracking**: Log completed exercises with timestamp history
- **Stats Dashboard**: Track daily, weekly, and streak progress
- **12-Week History**: Visual summary of your exercise consistency
- **Customizable Exercises**: Add, edit, or remove exercises from your list

## How It Works

1. Set your reminder interval (15-90 minutes)
2. When the timer hits zero, a modal pops up with a random exercise
3. Click "Start" to begin the 60-second countdown
4. Complete the exercise and click "Done!" to log it
5. Or click "Delay" or the X to postpone without logging

## Usage

Visit [buz2er.com](https://buz2er.com) to get started. All data is saved locally in your browser using localStorage.

## Privacy

All data stays local in your browser. No servers, no tracking, no analytics. You have full control over your exercise history and settings.

## Quick Actions

- **Exercise Now!** - Skip the countdown and start immediately
- **Delay** - Postpone the current reminder
- **Clear All Data** - Reset exercises, history, and settings

No installation required. No server needed. Just open and go.
